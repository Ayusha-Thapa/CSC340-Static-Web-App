Harry Potter

House: Gryffindor

Role: Student / Hero

Species: Wizard

Backstory

Harry Potter is known as the “Boy Who Lived” after surviving an attack from Lord Voldemort as a baby. He grows up with his aunt and uncle before learning that he is a wizard and attending Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Throughout the series, Harry faces many challenges and plays a major role in defeating Voldemort.

Abilities

  • Strong defensive magic
  • Skilled at Defense Against the Dark Arts
  • Quick decision-making in dangerous situations

Personality Traits

  • Brave
  • Loyal
  • Determined
  • Compassionate
